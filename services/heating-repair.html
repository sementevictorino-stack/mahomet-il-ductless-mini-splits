Common Heating Problems in Mahomet

Central Illinois winters test heating systems to their limits. Extended periods of below-freezing temperatures, frequent cycling, and high demand can lead to various heating problems requiring professional repair service:

  • No Heat Production: Complete heating failure due to ignition problems, gas supply issues, or electrical failures
  • Insufficient Heating: Reduced heating capacity caused by dirty filters, blocked vents, or worn components
  • Uneven Heating: Some rooms warm while others remain cold, indicating ductwork or airflow problems
  • Frequent Cycling: System turning on and off repeatedly, reducing efficiency and increasing wear
  • Strange Noises: Unusual sounds indicating mechanical problems or component failures
  • High Energy Bills: Increased heating costs due to inefficient operation or system problems
