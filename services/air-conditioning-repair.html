Common Air Conditioning Problems We Fix

Our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair all types of AC problems commonly experienced by Mahomet homeowners:

Cooling Performance Issues

  • AC Not Cooling: Complete loss of cooling capacity due to refrigerant leaks, compressor failure, or electrical problems
  • Insufficient Cooling: Reduced cooling performance often caused by dirty coils, low refrigerant, or airflow restrictions
  • Uneven Cooling: Some rooms too warm while others are comfortable, indicating ductwork or airflow issues
  • Short Cycling: System turning on and off frequently, reducing efficiency and increasing wear
  • AC Won't Start: Electrical problems, thermostat issues, or safety control activation preventing operation

Electrical and Control Problems

  • Thermostat Malfunctions: Incorrect temperature readings, programming issues, or communication failures
  • Electrical Connection Issues: Loose wiring, blown fuses, or circuit breaker trips
  • Capacitor Failures: Common electrical component failures preventing motors from starting
  • Control Board Problems: Electronic control issues affecting system operation
  • Safety Switch Activation: Safety controls shutting down system due to detected problems

Mechanical Component Failures

  • Compressor Problems: Heart of the AC system, expensive to replace but critical for cooling
  • Fan Motor Issues: Indoor or outdoor fan motors failing, reducing airflow and efficiency
  • Refrigerant Leaks: Loss of cooling capacity and potential environmental concerns
  • Coil Problems: Dirty or damaged evaporator and condenser coils affecting heat transfer
  • Drainage Issues: Clogged condensate drains causing water leaks and humidity problems

Central Air Conditioning Repair

Central air conditioning systems are common in Mahomet homes, providing whole-house cooling through ductwork. Our repair services cover all aspects of central AC systems:

Split System Repairs

Traditional split systems with outdoor condensing units and indoor evaporator coils require specialized expertise for diagnosis and repair. Common split system repairs include:

  • Refrigerant leak detection and repair
  • Compressor diagnosis and replacement
  • Evaporator and condenser coil cleaning
  • Fan motor replacement and repair
  • Electrical component replacement
  • Ductwork repair and sealing

Packaged Unit Repairs

Packaged air conditioning systems contain all components in a single outdoor unit. These systems require different diagnostic and repair approaches:

  • Complete system diagnosis in single unit
  • Refrigeration system repairs
  • Electrical system troubleshooting
  • Air handling component repair
  • Control system calibration
  • Weather protection and sealing

Heat Pump Cooling Repairs

Heat pumps provide both heating and cooling, but when operating in cooling mode, they can experience unique problems requiring specialized repair expertise:
