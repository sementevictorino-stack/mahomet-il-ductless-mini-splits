Furnace Installation Services

Central Illinois winters demand reliable heating systems. Our furnace installation services include high-efficiency gas furnaces, electric furnaces, and oil furnaces designed to keep your Mahomet home warm and comfortable even during the coldest weather.

Gas Furnace Installation

Natural gas furnaces remain the most popular heating choice for Mahomet homeowners due to their efficiency and operating costs. We install high-efficiency gas furnaces with AFUE ratings up to 98%, ensuring maximum comfort with minimal energy consumption. Our installation includes:

  • Proper furnace sizing based on detailed load calculations
  • Professional gas line connections and safety testing
  • Ductwork evaluation and modifications as needed
  • Thermostat installation and programming
  • Complete system commissioning and testing
  • Customer education on operation and maintenance

Electric Furnace Installation

For homes without natural gas service, electric furnaces provide reliable heating with precise temperature control. Modern electric furnaces offer excellent efficiency and can be paired with heat pumps for optimal energy savings in Mahomet's moderate climate.

Central Air Conditioning Installation

Beat Mahomet's hot, humid summers with professionally installed central air conditioning. We install energy-efficient systems that provide consistent cooling throughout your home while maintaining excellent humidity control.

Split System Air Conditioners

Traditional split system air conditioners remain popular for their reliability and performance. Our installation services include:

  • High-efficiency outdoor condenser units
  • Indoor evaporator coil installation
  • Refrigerant line installation and testing
  • Electrical connections and controls
  • Ductwork modifications for optimal airflow
  • Performance testing and optimization

Packaged Air Conditioning Systems

For homes with space constraints or specific installation requirements, packaged air conditioning systems offer an excellent solution. These units contain all components in a single outdoor unit, simplifying installation while providing excellent performance.

Heat Pump Installation

Heat pumps provide both heating and cooling with exceptional energy efficiency, making them ideal for Mahomet's moderate climate. Modern heat pumps can provide reliable heating even in sub-freezing temperatures, offering year-round comfort with lower operating costs.

Air Source Heat Pumps

Air source heat pumps extract heat from outdoor air and transfer it indoors during winter, reversing the process for summer cooling. Our installation includes:

  • Proper system sizing for heating and cooling loads
  • High-efficiency outdoor unit installation
  • Indoor air handler or coil installation
  • Backup heating system integration
  • Advanced control system programming
  • Performance optimization for local climate

Geothermal Heat Pumps

For maximum efficiency and environmental benefits, geothermal heat pumps use the earth's stable underground temperature for heating and cooling. While requiring higher initial investment, geothermal systems offer lowest operating costs and longest lifespan.

Ductless Mini Split Installation

As specialists in ductless mini split installation, we offer complete solutions for homes without existing ductwork or those requiring additional climate control zones. Ductless systems provide exceptional efficiency and individual room control.

Single Zone Systems

Perfect for room additions, converted spaces, or areas with specific comfort needs. Single zone ductless systems provide dedicated heating and cooling for individual spaces.

Multi-Zone Systems

Multi-zone ductless systems can replace traditional central HVAC systems while providing individual room control and enhanced efficiency. These systems are ideal for whole-home comfort solutions.

Hybrid System Installation

Hybrid systems combine multiple technologies for optimal efficiency and comfort. Common hybrid installations include:

  • Heat pump with gas furnace backup
  • Ductless mini splits with central system supplementation
  • Zoned systems with multiple technologies
  • Dual fuel systems for maximum efficiency
